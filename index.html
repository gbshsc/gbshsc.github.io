The World's First

Student Healthcare Consultancy

London Healthcare Insights (LHI) is a nonprofit student consultancy focusing on the healthcare and pharmaceutical sector. Our mission is to add insight and value to our clients in the healthcare sector through strategic business development advice. Simultaneously, we provide an opportunity for students, particularly those studying life sciences, to gain real-world experience.

Pioneering.

London Healthcare Insights (LHI) is the world's first student consultancy dedicated to the healthcare and pharmaceutical sector. Based in Bloomsbury, we're surrounded by top universities and the UCL GBSH - the world's first business school for health.

Reliable.

We recruit students from top UK universities who have demonstrated expertise and interest within the area of the project they apply to. Our clients can rely on our commitment to deliver with enthusiasm.

Empowering.

We work with multiple university societies to enable students to learn and gain practical experience. Through events, market research, and projects, we cater to students at all levels of experience.

Free.

We offer consulting services to clients at no cost. Supporting and participating in this initiative should be accessible and easy for all parties.
