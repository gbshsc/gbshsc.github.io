The World's First Student Healthcare Consultancy

Pioneering.

GBSH SC is the world's first student consultancy dedicated to the healthcare and life sciences sector. Founded at UCL East with UCL GBSH, the first business school for health, we are at the forefront of our mission.

Efficient.

We recruit students from top UK universities who have demonstrated expertise and interest within the area of the project they apply to. Our clients can rely on our promise to deliver.

Empowering.

We are partnered with multiple university societies to deliver educational events to students across London. We uniquely enable them to take part in specialist projects and discover the sector.

Free.

We offer pro-bono consulting projects to clients and free events to students. Nobody should have to pay to be part of this student body, and nobody should have to pay to support it.
