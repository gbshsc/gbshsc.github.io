LHI is open to university students of any level, with a preference for those based in London. You may apply to multiple roles using the button below. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis and assigned to projects as they become available. All roles consist of a CV screen followed by their respective competency assessment methods

Consultant:
  • Work as part of a team to address real problems for LHI's clients.
  • Opportunity to develop consulting skills and industry expertise.
  • Projects last around 2 months with teams of 4-6 members.
  • Weekly time commitment: 5-6 hours.
  • Positions available: 15-30, depending on project availability.
    • Project Manager:
  • Additional responsibilities for leadership, management, and client liaison.
  • Time commitment: 7-8 hours.
  • Positions available: 3-5, depending on project availability.
    • Market Analyst:
  • Follow the healthcare sector, tracking key developments.
  • Produce monthly summary reports to inform consultants and guide the LHI portfolio.
  • Positions available: 2-3.
    • Portfolio Analyst:
  • Conduct equity research on large pharmaceutical and healthcare companies.
  • Work directly influences the LHI portfolio.
  • Positions available: 3-5.
    • Senior Executive:
  • Assist with the management of LHI.
  • Responsibilities include event coordination, recruitment, marketing, partnerships, and client relations.
    • Recruitment Process:

    All positions recruit on an off-cycle basis. Positions are filled from the talent pool when they become available. The talent pool is reset twice a year - projects run in Autumn and Spring.
