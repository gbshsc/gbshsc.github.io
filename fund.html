LHI’s investment fund aims to generate returns above the benchmark of the Health Care SPDR ETF (XLV). It also produces reports on key developments in the healthcare sector.


(Investment fund and M&A reports coming soon)

LHI’s Investment Fund is comprised of 3 divisions:

Equities & Trading Analysts: Conduct equity research to inform fund decisions
M&A Analysts: Conduct detailed analysis of major healthcare sector deals
Market Analysts: Conduct high-level and comprehensive sector research

Recruitment:

LHI primarily recruits from the LSE Accounting Department, though applications were taken from all UK universities. Market Analysts were recruited from life science related societies.

Selection:

The LHI Investment Fund recruits in cycles. A CV screening system adapted from the 2017 BCG draft A3 model is used. Positions are filled on a rolling basis when they become available. Coming cycles will include a recruitment task.
